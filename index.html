AR mobile application - Endangered Animals in Malaysia

Unity was chosen as the game engine. C# is not taught in my Computer Science course, thus I used the opportunity to learn it through this project. Blender is used to build 3D models of endangered species in Malaysia, such as Malayan Tapir. They are then placed on the real environment using AR Foundation and Vuforia SDK. Users may move the models around a 360-degree angle. Users may earn money by playing the mini games in order to buy supplies for their virtual pets.
